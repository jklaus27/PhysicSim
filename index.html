What is this?

This is an interactive physics laboratory designed to let students discover physical relationships through experimentation rather than memorization. You will not see equations first.

Who is it for?

Middle school students, teachers, and anyone curious about physics as an experimental science.

Built as an open educational project. No accounts. No tracking of personal data.
